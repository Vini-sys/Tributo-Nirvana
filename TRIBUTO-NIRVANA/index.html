About tha band early years

Nirvana was a seminal American rock band formed in 1987 in Aberdeen, Washington . The band consisted of Kurt Cobain (vocals, guitar), Krist Novoselic (bass), and various drummers including Chad Channing and later Dave Grohl. Here's a brief summary of Nirvana's early years

Nirvana was formed by Kurt Cobain and Krist Novoselic after they met in Aberdeen, Washington in the mid-1980s. They bonded over their love of punk rock music..

The band went through several name changes before settling on Nirvana, inspired by the Buddhist concept of reaching enlightenment and liberation from suffering..

Nirvana's early sound was heavily influenced by punk and alternative rock bands of the time, such as the Pixies and Sonic Youth.

Curiosidade da Banda

  • In 1989, Nirvana released their debut album "Bleach" on the independent record label Sub Pop Records.
  • Nirvana's growing popularity led to major label interest, and in 1990 they signed with DGC Records, a subsidiary of Geffen Records.
  • Tragically, Nirvana's success was cut short by Kurt Cobain's untimely death in April 1994. His passing sent shockwaves through the music world and left a void that could never be filled
